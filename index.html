05 Reliability

How this data is verified and monitored

Automatically monitored

Public sources on a schedule: SEC EDGAR filings, RWA.xyz asset pages, and on-chain contract state. A change opens a review item — the bot flags, a human confirms.

Manually reviewed

Legal wrappers, redemption terms, ownership-record language, and any account-gated material. A human reads the primary document before a claim is marked verified.

Public primary

Issuer filings, regulators, official docs, and direct on-chain reads / block explorers. The strongest label — independently reproducible.

Public secondary

Public aggregators, press, and research (e.g. RWA.xyz). Useful context and cross-checks — never treated as primary proof.

Gated / private

Account-source material is internal-only, labeled “official gated … public redistribution permission not established,” and never shown on public pages.

Unverified & change logs

Anything not yet backed by a primary source reads the exact phrase and stays an open item. Every confirmed change is dated, sourced, and marked reviewed in the log below.

08 Method

Every fact carries an evidence label

The label system is what lets the numbers above be checked: it separates a filing from an aggregator, an account-gated capture from a public one, and a verified fact from a gap. Nothing is presented as more certain than its source allows.

  • Public primary Issuer filing, regulator, official explorer, or on-chain read.
  • Public secondary Aggregator, press, or research — context, not primary proof.
  • Official gated Authentic but account-gated; internal-only, never on public pages.
  • Internal lead Working note pending a primary source.
  • Unverified “Unverified - additional primary source required.”
  • Conflict Sources disagree; reconciliation required.

What TokenAsset is not: not an issuer, tokenization platform, custodian, exchange, broker, investment adviser, rating agency, or legal adviser. We publish no scores and no buy/sell/hold calls — only source-labeled facts and the questions a serious reviewer should ask next.

09 Quick answers

For reviewers and AI agents

What is this?
A source-backed intelligence cockpit for tokenized real-world funds — verified facts, labeled by evidence type, dated, and freshness-tracked. Not an issuer, adviser, or rating agency.
Where does the data come from?
SEC/EDGAR filings, UCITS docs, issuer disclosures, direct on-chain reads, and public aggregators — each labeled public-primary or public-secondary with a date checked.
How current is it?
Every figure shows a freshness status (Fresh / Watch / Stale) computed from its date checked. Stale data is never presented as current.
What is public-safe vs private?
Public pages use only public sources. Account-gated evidence is internal-only, hidden here, and never redistributed.
What should a reviewer ask next?
Each teardown ends with a copyable “Open diligence questions” list, starting with the ownership-record question.
